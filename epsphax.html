Step 2: Installing Adrenaline ePSP CFW


Part I: Requirements

  • The latest release of ePSP Bubble Installer (The VPK File).
  • The latest release of Adrenaline EasyInstaller (The VPK File).
  • Your PS Vita connected to the internet.

Part II: Installing ePSP Bubble Installer & Adrenaline EasyInstaller

  1. Open VitaShell
  2. Press Select to start the FTP Server.
  3. Press Start and choose your prefered SELECT button action (FTP Server / USB Connection)
  4. Connect to your Vita using your FTP client / USB Connection and navigate to 'ux0:'. (Default Directory w/ USB)
  5. Select 'ePSP_Bubble_Installer_vX.X.vpk' and press X. (vX.X may vary depending on version)
  6. Press X to install ePSP_Bubble_Installer.
  7. Press X at the warning box.
  8. Select 'EasyInstaller_vXXX.vpk' and press X. (vXXX may vary depending on version)
  9. Press X to install Adrenaline EasyInstaller.
  10. Press X at the warning box.
  11. Exit VitaShell


Part III: Installing ePSP Bubble & Adrenaline ePSP CFW

  1. Open 'ePSP Bubble Installer'.
  2. Press X to install an ePSP bubble. (This will reboot your Vita.)
  3. Wait for the bubble to be installed and your Vita to reboot.
  4. Rerun HENkaku either via the website or Offline Exploit
  5. Open 'EasyInstaller'
  6. Select 'Install Adrenaline eCFW'
  7. Select 'NPEZ00104 - BIGHEADSNOOKER'
  8. Choose 'Yes'
  9. Wait for Adrenaline to be installed.
  10. Press X to reboot.
  11. Wait for the database to update.
  12. Rerun HENkaku.
  13. Open 'EasyInstaller'
  14. Select 'Themeing Options'.
  15. Select 'Add Custom LiveArea Theme'.
  16. Choose 'Yes'.
    • If it tries to bring you to Adrenaline, choose 'Cancel' and go back to EasyInstaller, then press X to continue.
  17. Wait for it to finish and press X to reboot.
  18. Rerun HENkaku.
  19. Open 'Adrenaline'.
  20. Complete the PSP's Initial Setup.
  21. You now have CFW for the ePSP!
  22. To exit Adrenaline, Hold the PS button, tap Settings, and use the DPad + X to choose, 'Exit PspEmu Application'.
    • To play your PSP backups, put them in ux0:pspemu/ISO/.
    • To use your PSP saves with Adrenaline, copy the SAVEDATA folder from your PSP memory stick to ux0:pspemu/PSP/
